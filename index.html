About Our Foundation

Founded with a deep commitment to strengthening Ethiopian families, we believe that strong families are the foundation of thriving communities.

Our Story

Wegene Ethiopia Foundation was born from the understanding that families are the cornerstone of society. Our founders recognized that by supporting families, we create ripple effects that strengthen entire communities.

We work tirelessly to provide comprehensive family support services, from emergency assistance to long-term development programs that help families achieve self-sufficiency and prosperity.

Every family we help represents hope for a brighter future, not just for them, but for all of Ethiopia.

Our Mission

To strengthen Ethiopian families through comprehensive support, education, and community development initiatives.

Our Vision

A thriving Ethiopia where every family has the tools and support needed to build a prosperous future.

Our Values

Compassion, integrity, community partnership, and sustainable development guide everything we do.

Our Impact

Creating lasting change that transforms families and builds stronger, more resilient communities.

How We Help Families

Our comprehensive approach addresses the diverse needs of Ethiopian families through targeted programs and personalized support.

Housing Support

Helping families secure safe, affordable housing and providing assistance with home improvements and repairs.

Education Programs

Supporting children's education through scholarships, school supplies, and tutoring programs.

Healthcare Access

Connecting families with healthcare services, medical assistance, and health education programs.

Financial Assistance

Providing emergency financial support and helping families develop sustainable income sources.

Family Counseling

Offering family therapy, conflict resolution, and relationship building services.

Skills Training

Empowering families with vocational training and entrepreneurship development programs.

Need Help or Want to Help?

Whether you're a family in need or someone who wants to make a difference, we're here to connect and support each other.
